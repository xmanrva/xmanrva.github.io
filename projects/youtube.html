YouTube Offline Bypass

Attempting to watch YouTube videos on a school chromebook normally results in a screen saying "You are offline." Personally, I have no idea how this happens or why it does, but there is a bypass for it.

How to get around this block:
1. Click share on the YouTube video
2. Click "Embed" on the share page
3. Click "Enable privacy-enhanced mode"
4. Watch it from the page

Theory and History

This issue has been prevalent for a long time now, possibly a few months. A few months ago, this bug was easily fixed by adding "?t=0" to the end of the link. I discovered this on accident because I believed that it was clicking on the link from YouTube was causing it.

Recently, I discovered the trick no longer works, and the only solution was to download the video. I had given up due to not having any other solution, but I was shown a fix through the embed menu.

I don't use the YouTube embed menu, so thanks to this person, It can be fixed.
