Getting MC on a Chromebook

DISCLAIMER

I am not responsible for you getting in trouble for doing this. You are doing this discretion. It is not legal. Secondly, this will drain your whole battery in one day.

Step 1

Go to this website: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lax1dude/eaglercraft/main/stable-download/Offline_Download_Version.html

Right click, press "save as" and put it in your files

Step 2

Open the file from your files. You will be greeted with a screen saying it will open.

You can bookmark the file you've opened to save for later. After a bit of waiting, you have Minecraft.
